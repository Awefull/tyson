In Tyson, What We Sell

Rock wool board

Rock wool board uses natural basalt as raw material,It can be used in building walls, roofs, industrial furnaces, ovens, heat treatment equipment, storage tanks, storage tanks, power plants, flues, petrochemical, refining equipment

ROock wool blanket

Rock wool blankets are made of natural rocks such as basalt and dolomite. We can also provide various veneer materials such as aluminum glass cloth and aluminum foil according to customer needs. Suitable for industrial fields, mineral wool blankets are suitable for thermal insulation, noise reduction, and fire protection on uneven surfaces, containers, tank walls, pipes, etc. in power plants and refineries.

ROCK wool tube/pipe

Rock wool pipes are made of flocculent rock wool fiber as raw material, and can be provided with a variety of veneer materials such as aluminum foil and fiberglass cloth to meet different uses. The wired mat is especially suitable for industrial installations such as high-pressure steam pipes, reactors, furnaces, etc. where high demands are made on the temperature resistance of the insulation.

Glass Wool board

Glass wool board, a durable and compact insulation product made from fiberglass as raw material, is fireproof and waterproof and is widely used in heating and air conditioning ducts, electrical and processing equipment, boiler and chimney installations, metal and masonry Wall, wall and roof panel systems and curtain wall assemblies.

Glass Wool blanket

Glass wool roll is a high-quality thermal insulation and sound insulation material made of fiberglass. Aluminum foil surface, kraft paper surface, and white flame-retardant PVC veneer can be provided according to customer needs. It is widely used in metal building insulation, warehouse and greenhouse insulation and sound insulation, refrigeration equipment insulation, and vehicle shock absorption, sound absorption, and noise reduction.

Glass Wool tube/pipe

Glass wool pipe is an insulation material used for the insulation of pipes and other cylindrical objects in buildings and industrial facilities. Glass wool pipes can be customized in various diameters, thicknesses and lengths according to customer needs to accommodate different pipe sizes and applications. Insulation is often wrapped around pipes or inserted into preformed sheathing to create a tight fit
